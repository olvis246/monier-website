Who We Are

Built in Port Harcourt.
Working Across Nigeria.

Monier Construction Company (Nigeria) Limited is a full-service civil engineering and building contractor headquartered on Ikwerre Road, Port Harcourt. We handle both the heavy work — structural builds, demolition, and refurbishment — and the detail end: tiling, plumbing fixtures, drywall, painting, and finishing.

With a workforce in the thousands, our crews run multiple active sites simultaneously without compromising supervision or quality control. That scale lets us take on government infrastructure contracts and private renovation jobs alike.
